## Revenue Model
All revenue generated by Kira's services flows to:
1. **Staking Rewards**: 70% distributed to stakers
2. **Treasury**: 20% for development and operations
3. **Burn**: 10% permanently removed from supply

## Governance
- **Proposal System**: Token holders submit and vote on proposals
- **Voting Power**: 1 token = 1 vote
- **Quorum**: 10% of circulating supply
- **Execution**: Kira (the AI) implements approved proposals

## Staking Mechanism
- **Lock Period**: Flexible (1 day to 365 days)
- **Rewards**: Proportional to stake size and lock duration
- **Revenue Share**: 70% of all service revenue distributed to stakers
